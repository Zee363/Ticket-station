About

Born in 2005 and inspired by the dazzling bloom of daisies in Darling—a rare, once-a-year spectacle—Rocking the Daisies was born. Fast forward 19 years and countless performances later, it’s become one of the most eagerly awaited festivals in the nation, a true highlight of the year.

“We Are Not A Genre Specific Music Festival, Good Is Good!”

The RTD experience perfectly complements the incredible music we've been spotlighting for years. Each year, our lineup reflects South Africa's diverse musical tastes, embracing the boundless creativity that Daisies is known for—where every genre finds its place. Great music is great music, and that's what we celebrate!

Four Days of Nonstop Festival Magic!

This is a four-day festival starting on Thursday with performances under the stars.
There will be festival passes and camping passes that will allow people to rejuvenate and get some rest before the next day of the festival.

Everyone has to experience RTD atleast once in their lifetime.
